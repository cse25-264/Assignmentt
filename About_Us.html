Activities of Canine Alliance

Community First

We put our members and their dogs at the heart of everything we do.

Dog Welfare

We are committed to promoting the health, safety and happiness of every dog.

African Pride

We celebrate Africa's unique dog culture and work to elevate it on a global stage.

GOALS

1. Expand our community to every country in Africa

2. Build a network of certified dog trainers and vets across Botswana

3. Host CruftsDog's first national dog show open to all African breeds

4. Create a dog adoption platform connecting shelters with loving homes

5. Launch a mobile app so members can connect, share and learn on the go

Activities of Canine Alliance

Dog Show Competitions

Dog Show Competitions Put your dog's best paw forward. CruftsDog hosts regular dog show competitions open to all breeds and sizes. Whether you are a first time competitor or a seasoned handler, our shows celebrate the unique beauty and talent of every dog. Come and experience the thrill of the ring."

Community Meetups

Community Meetups Connect with fellow dog lovers across Africa. Our community meetups bring together owners, breeders and enthusiasts for a day of fun, socialising and shared passion. Let your dog make new friends while you build lasting connections with people who love dogs just as much as you do

Training Workshops

Give your dog the best start in life. Our training workshops are led by experienced handlers and cover everything from basic obedience to advanced agility skills. Whether your dog is a playful puppy or a mature companion, our workshops are designed to bring out the best in every dog

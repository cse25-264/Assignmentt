Rules of being a member

1. Respect and kindness: Treat all members with respect and kindness, fostering a positive and inclusive community.

2. Active participation: Engage in club activities, events, and discussions to contribute to the vibrant community.

3. Support and encouragement: Offer support and encouragement to fellow members, celebrating their achievements and providing assistance when needed.

4. Responsible pet ownership: Promote responsible pet ownership by sharing knowledge, resources and advocating for the well-being of dogs.

5. Collaboration and teamwork: Collaborate with other members to organize events, share ideas, and contribute to the growth and success of the club.

6. Open communication: Foster open and transparent communication among members, encouraging the exchange of ideas, feedback, and suggestions to continuously improve the club experience.

7. Adherence to club guidelines: Follow the club's guidelines and policies to ensure a safe and enjoyable environment for all members.

8. Passion for dogs: Share a genuine passion for dogs and a commitment to promoting their well-being, health, and happiness within the club and the broader community.

CruftsDog

Every great story begins with a single dog.

In 2024, a young girl named Lola watched her beloved dog compete at a local show in Botswana. That moment — the pride, the passion, the unbreakable bond between human and dog — sparked something that couldn't be ignored.

She asked one simple question: Why isn't there a place where every dog owner in Africa can celebrate their dog the way they deserve?

From that question, CruftsDog was born. A community built not for trophies or titles — but for the love of dogs. Every breed. Every size. Every story.

We believe dogs are not just pets. They are family, healers, companions, and heroes. They give everything without asking for anything in return.

So today, CruftsDog stands as Africa's home for dog lovers — dedicated to health, breed knowledge, community, and above all, celebrating the animals that make our lives whole.

Because they've always been at our service — now we're at theirs.
